VIRTUAL PET

Click here to go to Github

This was the project that taught me about inheritance, abstract classes, the importance of detail when naming.

The project is about Adopting a pet and give it a name. Check stats to make sure your pet has enough food, water and play time. Levels change every time an action occurs. These changes use a random number generator to make them unpredictable. If you feed your pet, thirst level goes up. If your pet is bored, take it out to the park. Playing will decrease boredom level and increase thirst level. Make sure levels stay healthy. Hi boredom levels could make your puppy sad. High thirst or hunger levels could bring Animal Services to rescue your pet.

Software: Microsoft Visual Studio
Language: C#
